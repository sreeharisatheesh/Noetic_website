Noetic is an AI keyboard that fixes grammar, rewrites your tone, translates to 16 languages, and summarizes text — without leaving the app you're in.

FIX GRAMMAR

Perfect every sentence.

Select any text, tap Fix, and Noetic rewrites it for clarity, grammar, and flow. Two modes: Fix Grammar for corrections, Refine for polished rewrites.

  • Corrects grammar & punctuation
  • Refine mode for stylistic polish
  • One tap — no copy-pasting

TONE REWRITE

23 tones. Any vibe.

Switch between Professional, Casual, Gen Z, Flirty, Scholarly, and 18 more — directly in your keyboard.

  • 23 fully customisable tones
  • Reorder and personalise your palette
  • Works in every text field

AI ASSIST

Do more than fix. Create.

Ask questions, generate smart auto-replies, humanize text, expand ideas, shorten messages, paraphrase, and more — all from within your keyboard.

  • Auto Reply with sentiment control
  • Humanize, Expand, Shorten, Paraphrase
  • Premium: Hook, Viral, Pitch, and more

SUMMARIZE

Long messages, instantly short.

Paste any block of text and get a clean bullet-point summary in seconds.

  • Long messages → clean bullets
  • Paste from anywhere, summarize in-keyboard
  • Works on articles, emails, threads
