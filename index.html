Works in every app.

Three simple steps to better writing, wherever you are.

⌨

Switch keyboards

Tap the globe icon on any iOS keyboard to switch to Noetic. Works instantly in Messages, Gmail, WhatsApp, Instagram — anywhere.

✦

Tap your tool

Choose Fix, Tone, Assist, Summarize, or Translate. Noetic only processes text when you tap an action — never passively reading what you type.

◈

Replace or copy

Your text is rewritten in seconds. Tap Replace to insert it directly, or Copy to use it anywhere.

We never read your keyboard.

Full Access is needed for AI network requests — not for logging. Passwords, messages, and browsing are never accessible to us.

🔒

No keystrokes logged

We only process text when you explicitly tap a button. Passive monitoring is impossible by design.

🛡

Passwords never seen

iOS isolates password fields completely. Our keyboard cannot access them.

🗑

Text not stored

Your text is processed for the AI request and immediately discarded. We retain nothing.

🔐

Encrypted everywhere

All communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Your data is protected in transit.
